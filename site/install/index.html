YT Music Companion

Plug the board into this computer with a USB cable, then click Install.

Your browser can't flash the board — use Chrome or Edge on a desktop computer. Serial access was blocked. Reload and allow it.
  1. Click Install and pick the board's serial port.
  2. Wait for flashing to finish (~1 minute).
  3. Enter your 2.4 GHz Wi-Fi name and password when asked.
  4. The board reboots and connects. Play a song on ytmdesktop.

Requires Chrome or Edge on desktop (Web Serial). Safari, Firefox, and iOS aren't supported.
